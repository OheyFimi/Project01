Secret Recipe

Description


This apple pie recipe has been in my family for generations and nevers fails to delight.

Ingredients


  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 2 tablespoon of ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium sized of apples, peeled and sliced

    • Instructions


  • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • In a bowl, mix the sugar and cinnamon.
  • Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamon mixture.
